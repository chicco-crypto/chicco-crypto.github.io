About Me


Hi my name is Nicole! First of all I would like to thank you for taking the time to visit my blog page. I hope you find the content here useful.

I am a recent Ngee Ann Polytechnic graduate with a background in Cyber Security and Digital Forensics. I excelled in the area of Network Security and I do enjoy Vulnerability and Penetration Testing as well.

In my free time I make YouTube content to share my work and tutorials of what I learned and some troubleshooting videos. One such example you might find is the usage of CISCO Packet Tracer.

If I am feeling playful I participate in Capture the Flag (CTF) Competitions or just practice CTF in my own time using platforms like HackTheBox and TryHackMe.

My goals this year are to finish my CompTIA Security+ and EC-Council CEH Certifications. As for long term goals, I am deeply passionate about Network Security and would like to work as a security engineer or architect in the future.
